Behind the work
About

Design first. Tools second. Outcomes always.

I'm from Sopore, a small town in North Kashmir — a place better known for apple orchards than design studios. I taught myself the craft far from any creative hub, turned curiosity into a profession, and spent seven years growing from a freelance designer into a senior creative trusted by global teams. The distance never mattered; the work did.

I'm a senior creative who treats every brief as one continuous problem — what a brand says (identity, campaigns, presentation systems), what it shows (motion graphics, film, photoreal 3D), and what it lets people do (real-time, interactive, gamified experiences).

That range was built deliberately: brand systems and 30+ international client decks at a global engineering consultancy; then production-grade Unreal Engine 5 experiences, Blueprint logic, and AI-assisted pipelines at an immersive-tech team. I direct the idea and build the thing.

I'm open to remote, hybrid and on-site roles — I've worked across all three and adapt easily. I lead without needing to be managed, and hold one standard everywhere: if it doesn't communicate, it doesn't ship.

Good to know

Where is Zuhaib based, and what working styles has he handled?

He's based in Delhi NCR, India, and is open to remote, hybrid or on-site work. He started out in Sopore, North Kashmir, and has since worked on-site and hybrid across Delhi NCR and Hyderabad, and remotely with international clients — so he's comfortable across very different setups, teams and time zones.

Can you take a project from concept all the way to delivery?

Yes — that's the core of how I work. Strategy, art direction, design, 3D, motion and final production are all handled by one person, so the original idea survives intact from brief to finished asset.

What disciplines and tools do you actually cover?

Brand identity and visual systems, motion graphics and video, photoreal 3D visualization, and real-time interactive work in Unreal Engine 5 — using the full Adobe suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, Premiere Pro), Blender, Figma, Substance Painter and AI-assisted workflows.

Have you worked with international teams and clients?

Yes. I've delivered 30+ decks and campaigns for international clients at a global consultancy, and produced real-time and 3D work for an immersive-tech team — across a range of industries, cultures and working styles.

How do you use AI in your process?

As an accelerator, not a replacement. AI tools speed up ideation, concepting and iteration so more time goes into craft and direction — typically cutting concept-to-delivery time by around a third while keeping the final quality fully hand-controlled.
