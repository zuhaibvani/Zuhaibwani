Privacy & Data

Last updated: June 2026

This is a personal portfolio site for Zuhaib Wani. It is intentionally simple, and it collects as little as possible. This page explains exactly what happens with any information you choose to share.

What is collected

The only place this site collects personal data is the contact form. If you choose to send a message, you may provide:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your email address (optional)
  • The message you write

If you do not use the contact form, no personal data is collected.

Who processes it

Form submissions are delivered through Web3Forms (api.web3forms.com), a third-party form-handling service, which forwards your message to Zuhaib's email inbox. Your data passes through Web3Forms purely to deliver the message. See the Web3Forms privacy policy for how they handle data in transit.

Why it is collected

Solely so that Zuhaib can read your message and reply to you. Your details are never sold, rented, or used for marketing, advertising, or profiling.

The lawful basis for this processing is legitimate interest — replying to enquiries you choose to send — under Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR.

How long it is kept

Messages live in Zuhaib's email inbox for as long as needed to respond and keep a record of the conversation. You can ask for yours to be deleted at any time.

Cookies & analytics

This site uses Vercel Web Analytics to collect anonymized traffic data (page views, referrer, browser type, country) to help improve the site. This is a privacy-friendly service that sets no cookies and does not track individual users across sites. See the Vercel Analytics privacy policy for details.

No advertising or tracking cookies are set, and nothing follows you around the web because of a visit here.

Your rights

You can ask to access, correct, or delete any personal data you have sent, or withdraw a request, by emailing Zuhaibmushtaq95@gmail.com. Reasonable requests are honoured promptly.
